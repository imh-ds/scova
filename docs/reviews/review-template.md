## Checklist

- [ ] Parameter and assumptions are complete.
- [ ] Outcome, assignment, and covariate tangent channels are correct.
- [ ] The candidate gradient lies in the tangent space and has mean zero.
- [ ] One-step expansion and nuisance-rate claims are justified.
- [ ] Finite-grid multiplier result is stated at the proven strength.
- [ ] Sign and stability certificate claims follow from the joint event.
- [ ] Fixed-target and binary-overlap reductions are correct.
- [ ] Theory-to-code notation map is accurate.
- [ ] Public documentation contains no stronger claim.
